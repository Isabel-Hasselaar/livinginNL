Basic Information

The Netherlands, also known as Holland, is a country in the North West of Europe known for tulips, windmills, cycling, canals, and other important landmarks. The capital city is Amsterdam and there are about 17.28 people living there. The currency is Euro and the official language is Dutch, but everyone speaks English as well.
